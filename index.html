Day 1 - The Maze

When you first go to Niagra Falls, you most likely will see the different types of activies that are offered there. On the first day that I went there, I remember the Maze Mystery challange. You had to find your way through a maze and find the Mystery Sword. It tested your direction skills.

Day 2 - The Horseshoe Fall

During this boat ride, you get to see the Horseshoe fall! You will first be greeted and guided to a boat and get handed a raincoat. Then you will get a tour of the horseshoe fall. It's very beauiful, and the experiece is echancing.

Day 3 - The Food

There are many amazing food stores to try out in Niagra falls! You can get a wide range of muiliple cultural foods. From sushi, to fast food, even outsdie street vendors selling snacks. Try a wide verity of these foods and see which you enjoy!
